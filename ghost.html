Ghost

They call me Ghost. Rightly so. You don't see me. You don't hear me. You only see what's left after I'm gone. I'm the bloke they send in when the line gets blurry... when a message needs to be sent without a return address. Don't try to know who I am. Just know what I do. And pray you're not on my list. Out.
